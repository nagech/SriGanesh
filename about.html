    About Sri Ganesh Electricals

    We at Sri Ganesh Electricals” established 2007, is a leading and well recognized name as manufacturer, supplier of Electrical control panels, Power distribution boards, Bus ducts, Circuit breakers and other instrumentation support for various industries. With its business operations based at Hyderabad, Telangana & Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, India, the company has with it more than a decade of industry experience that assists it to successfully understand and deliver end power instrumentation solutions as required by the customers. Its business operations are led by Mr. P. Veeranjaneyulu, who has with him rich experience in the work area of LT & HT Bus Ducts, Control Panels, Switchyard, Relay Panels, Distribution Boards and more. His vision and dedicated support provided by the team has helped the company to successfully emerge as a leading Electrical Panel Builder from Hyderabad, India.

    Currently, “Sri Ganesh Electricals” is successfully meeting the upcoming challenges in the field of Industrial, Residential & Commercial, Electrification. With expertise in designing, manufacturing, supplying, erection and commissioning of LT Electrical Power Distribution Panels up to 33KV among other solutions, the company has successfully served the power instrumentation needs of many industry sectors. Some of the work sectors it has touched include Cement, Spinning, Sugar, Power, Bio-Technology, Effluent Treatment and others.
